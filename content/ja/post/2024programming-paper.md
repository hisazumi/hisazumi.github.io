## 論文情報

**Harumi Watanabe, Nobuhiko Ogura, Kenji Hisazumi**, "Towards a DevOps Modeling based on Context-Oriented Programming," The International Conference on the Art, Science, and Engineering of Programming (Programming) 2024, 2024.
